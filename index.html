Our Mission

To empower women and children through comprehensive programs that promote education, health, skill development, and gender equality for sustainable community transformation.

Saroj Vandana Children and Women Development is dedicated to empowering rural women and girls through skill-based training, health awareness, and livelihood opportunities.
